Our Story

Built by a dad,
for his kids.

Hatmaker Apps started with a simple idea — take the classic games my kids love to play on the living room table and put them in their hands anywhere we go.

Every game we build is designed to be simple enough for young kids but engaging enough that the whole family wants to play. No distractions, no dark patterns, just good games.

We're based in Idaho and publish all our apps under JEH Electronics on Google Play.
