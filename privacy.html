01 About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Android applications developed and published by Hatmaker Apps (a brand of JEH Electronics) on the Google Play Store.

By downloading or using any of our applications, you agree to the terms described in this policy. JEH Electronics is registered in Idaho, USA.

02 Current Free Apps — No Data Collected

Our currently available applications — Hangman, Sink It!, and Code Breaker — do not collect, request, store, transmit, or share any personal information of any kind, including:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifiers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, media, contacts, or files from your device

Any game data such as scores or settings is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere.

03 Future Paid Apps & Apps That Collect Data

As our catalog grows, we may release applications that are paid, offer in-app purchases, require user accounts, or collect certain data to deliver their features. When that happens:

  • This privacy policy will be updated with a dedicated section for that app before it launches
  • The app's Google Play Store listing will clearly state what data it collects
  • Data collection will be limited to what is necessary to provide the app's features
  • Users will be informed within the app before any data is collected

Payment processing: For paid apps or in-app purchases, all transactions are handled by Google Play. Hatmaker Apps does not receive or store your payment card details. Google's handling of payment data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

04 How We May Use Data (Future Apps)

For any future app that collects personal data, that data may be used solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the app's features
  • Manage user accounts and authenticate access
  • Respond to support requests or user feedback
  • Send important notices related to the app, such as policy updates

We will never sell personal data to third parties. We will never use personal data for advertising purposes without explicit user consent.

05 Third-Party Services

Our current free apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection SDKs.

Future apps may integrate third-party services such as authentication providers or cloud storage. Any such integrations will be disclosed in that app's specific section of this policy when added.

The Google Play Store may collect information about your device and app usage in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This is outside our control.

06 Children's Privacy

Our current games are designed for the whole family including young children. Because our current apps collect no personal data from anyone, they comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

For any future app that may be used by children under 13, we will take additional steps to ensure COPPA compliance, including obtaining verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information.

07 Data Retention & Deletion

For our current free apps, no personal data is retained by us because none is collected.

For any future app that collects personal data, we will retain that data only as long as necessary to provide the app's services or as required by law. Users will have the ability to request deletion of their account and associated data, consistent with Google Play's requirements.

08 Data Security

For our current free apps, there is no personal data on our servers to protect. Game data stored locally on your device is subject to your Android device's own security features.

For any future app that stores personal data on our servers, we will implement industry-standard security measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data involved.

09 Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy when we release new apps, change our data practices, or when required by law. The Effective Date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

For material changes — such as a new app beginning to collect personal data — we will provide clear notice within the affected application before the change takes effect. Continued use of our applications after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
